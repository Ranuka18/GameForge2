Premium Speed Pass

The Premium Speed Pass offers an incredible opportunity to unlock premium content in NFS Unbound with 30 additional tiers of rewards. It includes exclusive items such as Lockdown Spec Customs, Manufacturer Projections, the iconic NFS Legends Subaru BRZ 2015, and vibrant Graffiti Driving Effects. With this pass, players can elevate their racing experience through unique customizations and legendary rides, making their presence truly unforgettable on the streets.

Prepare for Lockdown

The Prepare for Lockdown mode introduces an exhilarating PvPvE challenge where teams of up to three players battle each other, the Lakeshore police, and an ever-shrinking Lockdown Zone. Players can break into Lockups to claim Hot Rides, secure loot using the Extraction Truck, or experience the thrill of riding the BMW S 1000 RR 2019 motorbike in a groundbreaking NFS feature. This mode includes exciting content like new rides—the Subaru Impreza WRX STI 1999 and Honda Integra Type R 1998—NFS 30th Anniversary Customs, and a robust Speed Pass offering 45 free tiers filled with amazing rewards
